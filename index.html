Be part of history


Bitcoin was born out of the financial crash of 2008 and proposed a brave new solution to the global problems in the shape of a new, decentralised economy. Tax payers bailed out private institutions and central banks undertook a period of unprecendented money printing to reliquidate the markets.
We're living in a period of economic experiment for which there is no viable exit strategy.
Bitcoin caught the attention of many initially as a kind of 'digital gold' something that poses to hold a store of value as fiat currencies collapse under extreme inflation due to their infinite supply.
Businesses then very quickly recognised the potential of the underlying blockchain ledger and money has been flooding into the market ever since.
Huge industry names such as Microsoft, Toyota, JP Morgan and many more are flocking to the technology and within the market we're already seeing firm favourites and big winners.
