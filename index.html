About us

Blockchain Consult couples blockchain services to enterprise needs.

Focusing on: Logistics, Cold-chain logistics, Automobile, Medical & Healthcare, Luxury & Fashion, Food & Drink and Agriculture we provide a very direct route to the most effective blockchain solutions on the market.

Utilising the experience of lead consultant Stephen Molyneux, we guide businesses and individuals who want to know more about the technology and the market but simply don't know where to start.


‘I’ve been in crypto since 2013 and was an early investor of Bitcoin, Litecoin and Ethereum among many others and I have watched the market evolve and steadily become more main street and reputable.

It's a vibrant space and there's no shortage of drama and risk and having salvaged my funds from MtGox and Cryptsy I recognise the pitfalls that the market can present. Blockchain Consult lets businesses and individuals alike make use of my experience and find a secure route to approaching and entering the market.

Blockchain technologies are the future and we're continually looking for the next Amazon and Google to rise up from this incredibly dynamic market.’

Stephen Molyneux
