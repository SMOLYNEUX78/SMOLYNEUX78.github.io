About us

Blockchain Consult is a service designed to help people approach the blockchain market. Utilising the experience of lead consultant Stephen Molyneux, we aim to guide individuals and businesses who want to know more but don’t have the time or technical acumen to filter through the abundance of information online

‘I’ve been in crypto since 2013 and was an early investor in Bitcoin, Litecoin, Ripple and Ethereum. I salvaged my funds from MtGox and Cryptsy and was an investor in DAO which subsequently led to the era of hard forks, so I know about the risks and pitfalls that are present in the market.
I’ve been advising friends and colleagues on cyrpto for years now so what with the current ground swell in the market, I thought to offer my services to a broader audience so as to make sure new investors aren’t misled.
Blockchain technologies are the future and we are continually looking for the next Amazon and Google to rise up from this ever evolving market.’

Stephen Molyneux

Service

Entry


Where to start?
Well you're here, so you've probably already read about cryptocurrencies, maybe researched certain projects that catch your eye and are ready to jump in.

Our consultation service is designed to meet your level of need on entering the market. Whether you're a business or an individual, we will tailor a system for you. Whether you want to enter the market with a holding of Bitcoin alone or a portfolio of alts we will map out a simple, risk free route to owning and securing your cryptocurrencies.

Security



We provide a simple, tried and tested system for individuals and businesses to buy, hold and trade their holdings in the most secure way.

We give a full induction to the platforms you will be utilising and will provide technical advice once you're set up. We have a range of options available to get into cryptocurrencies best tailored to your needs and confidence with technology

Guidance
