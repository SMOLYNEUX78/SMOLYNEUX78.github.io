About Us

Hello

Blockchain Consult couples enterprise needs with blockchain solutions

Utilising the insight and industry connections we have compiled since 2013 we guide businesses and individuals looking to find out more about blockchain tech

Focusing on:

Logistics

Being one of the lowest hanging fruits for blockchain tech, logistics is a key area for us. There are some solutions out there for logistics companies looking to utilise blockchain, however, not all will be fit for purpose

Automobile

The car is about to get smarter. Being able to provide your vehicle's historical records and carbon output with complete immutability will soon be a reality across the market.

Medical & Healthcare

Tracking the end to end production of medical devices on the blockchain improves their efficacy and contributes to improved healthcare and lives saved

Luxury & Fashion

If you have a premium product you need to have a digital certificate of provenance. Reassure your clients and imrpove their experience of your brand

Food & Drink

From the vine to the glass, the journey of all food and drink processes can be tracked on the blockchain. This presents a powerful tool for distributors and logistics providers and also gives the value of reassurance to consumers

Cold-chain logistics

Proprietry IoT devices can be utilised to track important metrics making cold chain logistics more robust and auditable

Agriculture

Blockchain enabled cloud services can provide certification services for organic food. Farming data can be stored and utilised to improve yield and performance
